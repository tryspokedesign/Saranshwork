I'm Saransh, a Webflow Developer and Javascript Expert

My journey began 9 years ago at garage startups as a Full Stack Developer helping creating pixel perfect and smooth experiences to the users. That enabled my interest in design and eventually into Webflow working with cross functional teams across India, USA, UK and UAE.
